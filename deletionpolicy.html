IBChannel Panel: Deletion Policy

If you would like to delete your Personal Information from our servers, please contact us at contact: contact@infinitybrains.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable time-frame and will require you to verify your identity before we can process your request. Once we have verified your identity, we will delete your Personal Information from our servers, except where we are required by law to retain certain information.

Please note that we may retain certain information necessary for us to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements. We may also retain certain information if it is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as fraud prevention and enhancing the safety and security of our Service.

If you delete your account, we will retain your Personal Information for a reasonable period of time to ensure that we can still respond to any inquiries or claims related to your account. After this period, we will delete your Personal Information, except where we are required by law to retain certain information.

If you have any questions or concerns about our deletion policy, please contact us at contact@infinitybrains.com.
